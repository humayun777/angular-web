Ghee Products

We are also committed to provide you 100% pure cow milk ghee which has been made in house; The Ghee has also been tested in lab as well as sample homes. The Ghee has been made using traditional Vedic method and not using giant machines and chemicals unlike other ghee company. We are #makingRANCHIhealthier.

Pure

It has been made using only cow milk. With zero impurities and distinct aroma and flavor it makes our ghee best in the market.

Health

It is said that 1 spoon of cow ghee daily can boost your immune system, and pure ghee can do it better as compared to artificial ghee. Not all ghee is good for health, if you consume large amount of hydrogenated ghee (Buffalo Ghee) will lead to thickening of arteries, accumulation of fats in body and decreased metabolism.

Organic

No chemicals are used for the production of the ghee, 60% of the fodder given to the cow is grown in house and those cows are responsible for the pure milk used to make pure organic ghee.

Making

It has been prepared using traditional Vedic method which starts with preparation of curd then separation of cream from that followed by heating and then filtrations which give birth to pure and organic ghee.
